Why Join CSC?

Learn Industry Skills

Master ethical hacking, penetration testing, digital forensics, and cybersecurity frameworks used by industry professionals.

Build Network

Connect with like-minded students, industry professionals, and alumni who share your passion for cybersecurity.

Compete & Win

Participate in CTF competitions, hackathons, and cybersecurity challenges to test your skills and win prizes.

What We Offer
01

Hands-on Workshops

Regular workshops on penetration testing, malware analysis, network security, and the latest cybersecurity tools.

02

Expert Guest Lectures

Learn from industry experts, security researchers, and ethical hackers through exclusive guest lectures and seminars.

03

CTF Training

Specialized training for Capture The Flag competitions, including web security, cryptography, and reverse engineering.

04

Research Projects

Participate in cutting-edge cybersecurity research projects and contribute to academic publications.

05

Mentorship Program

Get paired with senior members and alumni for personalized guidance and career development.

Who Can Join?

Basic Requirements

  • Current student at Uttara University
  • Passionate about cybersecurity and technology
  • Commitment to attend regular meetings and events
  • Willingness to learn and collaborate

Preferred (Not Required)

  • Basic knowledge of programming (Python, JavaScript, etc.)
  • Understanding of networking concepts
  • Experience with Linux/Command line
  • Previous participation in hackathons or CTFs
